About Us

Mandtra Residences is a modern urban development designed to bring comfort, convenience, and style into everyday living. Nestled in the heart of Mandaue City, it offers a perfect balance between accessibility and relaxation — close to major establishments, yet surrounded by a serene, community-focused environment. Featuring thoughtfully designed units and resort-style amenities, Mandtra Residences lets you experience the best of city life while enjoying the warmth of home.
